Welcome to Willow & Paw Photography


Where muddy paws, wagging tails, and real moments become memories you’ll treasure forever.

I specialise in relaxed, outdoor dog photography that captures your dog at their very best — guided gently into natural positions and situations that suit them, without ever forcing or rushing the moment. Sessions are calm, flexible, and shaped around your dog’s personality, energy, and comfort.

If we need to pause, explore, or take a break, that’s absolutely fine. Your dog’s wellbeing always comes first — because the most beautiful photographs happen when they feel safe, happy, and themselves.
