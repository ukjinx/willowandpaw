About Joe Jenkinson Photography

Welcome to my website, where passion meets the lens!

Since 2008, I have been on a journey behind the camera, capturing the essence of the world’s landscapes and wildlife.

I am Joe Jenkinson, a dedicated landscape and wildlife photographer based in the heart of the UK.

The Journey Behind the Lens

My photographic journey began in 2008, sparked by a deep appreciation for the great outdoors.

Armed with a camera, I set out to capture the awe-inspiring landscapes and fascinating wildlife that grace our planet.

Over the years, this journey has taken me from the rolling hills and serene lakes of the UK to the far reaches of distant lands, always driven by the desire to freeze moments in time and tell stories through my images.

Photography as a Path to Wellbeing

Beyond its creative expression, photography has been a powerful tool in my own mental health journey.

After struggling with depression for many years, I found solace behind the camera. Being out in nature—walking among trees, mountains, and open skies—brings a profound sense of peace and calm, allowing me to leave my worries behind.

Photography has become my form of mindfulness; it grounds me in the present moment, where I can focus solely on the art of capturing what’s in front of me.

Through this practice, I’ve discovered a therapeutic escape, and I hope my work inspires others to find similar peace and joy.

The Great Outdoors as My Muse

There’s an indescribable joy that comes from being in nature, and it’s this connection that fuels my creativity.

Whether it’s the soft glow of dawn painting the sky in hues of pink and orange or the raw power of a wild animal in its natural habitat, I strive to capture the essence of each moment.

Beyond Boundaries

While my roots are firmly planted in the UK, my lens knows no boundaries.

I believe that beauty is universal, and my travels have allowed me to witness and document the diverse landscapes and wildlife that make our planet truly extraordinary.

From the rugged cliffs of the British coastline to the untamed wilderness of distant continents, each photograph tells a unique story.

Join Me on This Journey

Photography is not just about taking pictures; it’s about sharing experiences and emotions.

Through my lens, I invite you to join me on a visual journey that transcends time and space.

Explore the galleries, lose yourself in the colors and textures, and let the images speak to your soul.

Thank you for visiting Joe Jenkinson Photography.

I hope my work inspires you to see the world through a different lens.

Capturing Moments,
Joe Jenkinson
