How a session works

Before the session

Once you’ve booked, we’ll have a chat about your dog — their personality, energy levels, and anything you’d like captured. Based on this, I’ll suggest a suitable outdoor location in South East Wales that fits your dog and the kind of images you’re drawn to.

No pressure, no rigid plans — just a shared understanding before we meet.

On the day

We’ll meet at the chosen location and simply go for a walk. This gives your dog time to get comfortable with me and their surroundings.

As we walk, I’ll look for natural locations where we can pause briefly. I’ll gently guide you when needed, always keeping things natural and relaxed.

  • Slow-paced and relaxed
  • Led by your dog’s comfort
  • On lead or off lead — your choice

During the session

Sessions usually last around 60-90 minutes, but I don’t keep track of time. Your dog’s wellbeing always comes first.

I focus on quality over quantity — capturing expression, character, and the quiet moments in between.
